What we're doing

With a group composed entirely of students from the University of Coimbra, we are developing a project to reduce food waste by making use of the excess food produced in faculty institutions.

Taking an unique approach on this industry, we aim to provide a service that is both beneficial to the environment and to the people, while never leaving our core principles behind.

Here are some of the things that make us stand out:

Low cut fees to ensure the best prices for our users. Achieved by taking support from social grants and other government programs.

Frequent reavaluation of our partners and in person facility check-ups to ensure quality is maintained.

24/7 chat support for any questions you may have.

Quarterly donations to charities that help fight hunger all around the world, with the money you spend with us.

Some common questions we get asked!

How many locations are you partnered with?

We are always expanding our network and striving to work with more and more instutions and their respective facilities. You will be able to see a full list of avaliable locations when ordering from our final product.

I'm not a faculty member, can I still order?

Yes, our service is not limited to faculty members. We do however give special offers to those enrolled in the higher education setting, so you will be missing out on this.

Will I be assured that the food is up to my standards?

Of course, we only work with instutions that have dedicated nutritionists planning their meals. We will also feature a rating system for each location so you can see what other people think.

How much will I be saving by ordering from you?

The amount you save depends on the location of your pickup but you're guaranteed to have a discount of at least 20% compared to the original price. And you'll be helping the environment!
